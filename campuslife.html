Life at IIM Bodh Gaya

A vibrant ecosystem of diverse minds, cultural richness, and rigorous learning

An Epitome of Versatility

Going to a B-School where you are taught by some of the best faculty in the country and surrounded by diverse minds makes campus life spectacularly perfect.

Right from its inception, IIM Bodh Gaya has maintained an environment that imbibes broader and rational thinking. To nurture these qualities, the institute offers a compatible environment for business interactions, cultural activities, and sports competitions. Top leaders from the industry frequently visit to share insights through B-Talk events.

Academic & Learning Ambience

Rigorous Education

IIMBG follows one of the rigorous curriculum practices of top IIMs. Fruitful classroom discussions with diverse student perspectives make learning interesting. Despite the stressful and hectic academic schedule—working day in and day out on projects, late-night group activities, and surprise quizzes—the journey shapes you into a competent professional.

Modern Facilities

Being in a place of enlightenment, the institute focuses heavily on the learning ambience. Classrooms are air-conditioned and equipped with state-of-the-art technology to enhance learning. Faculty doors are always open for guidance in career building and competitions. Students often form study groups to ace exams in this unique ecosystem.

Accommodation (Hostels)

Student Hostels

As you enter the hostel gates, you notice the basketball and volleyball courts bustling with students. The heart of the hostel is the common hall, where students play table tennis, watch sports, or discuss assignments.

  • Gym: Located exactly above the common room, equipped with state-of-the-art equipment.
  • Rooms: Single or double occupancy based on preference.
  • Amenities: Water coolers and common washrooms in each corridor.

Gargi Hostel (Women)

The exclusive women's hostel spans three floors with well-lit, ventilated rooms equipped with beds, mattresses, study tables, and cupboards.

  • Sports: Basketball, volleyball, and badminton courts inside the premises.
  • Facilities: In-house gym, huge common room for indoor games, and separate washing machines.
  • Connectivity: Fully connected via WiFi and LAN.
  • Dining: Mess facility located inside the hostel building.

Beyond Academics

Cultivating skills, hobbies, and lifelong bonds

Cultural Life

Birthday celebrations that turn loneliness into family, and culturally diverse festivals that bestow unity in diversity. Hobbies like dancing, painting, singing, and stand-up comedy thrive here.

Sports Spirit

Setting up systems from scratch imparts a great learning curve. Each day starts with a new struggle and a new hope. The journey is exhilarating, with facilities for basketball, volleyball, cricket, and more.

Serene Environment

The campus boasts calm surroundings and a pollution-free atmosphere. Students enjoy leisurely walks, explore food in Bodh Gaya city, and visit the Bodhi Temple for tranquility.

About IIM Bodh Gaya

Indian Institute of Management Bodh Gaya is committed to creating socially responsible managers and emotionally mature, mindful leaders who can make meaningful contributions to society.
