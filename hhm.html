Highlights

A two-year full-time residential program designed to create future leaders for the healthcare industry.

Healthcare Focus

Specialized tracks in Hospital Management and Healthcare Management (Public Health, Insurance, Pharma).

Industry Immersion

4-month extended industry immersion to work on live projects in hospitals or pharma companies.

Community Project

Field study in semi-urban/rural areas to understand public health issues and government schemes.

General Electives

Flexibility to choose electives from HR, Marketing, Finance, IT, and Operations domains.
