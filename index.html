About IIM Bodh Gaya

Excellence in Management Education

The Indian Institute of Management Bodh Gaya (IIM-BG) was established in 2015 as the 16th Indian Institute of Management. Located in the spiritual land of Bodh Gaya, Bihar, the institute is mentored by the prestigious IIM Calcutta.

Under the leadership of Director Dr. Vinita S. Sahay (appointed 2018), IIM-BG has consistently grown its academic excellence and research output. The institute is committed to creating socially responsible managers and emotionally mature, mindful leaders who can make meaningful contributions to society.

With a strong focus on holistic development, IIM-BG offers a unique blend of rigorous academics, mindfulness practices, and social immersion programs that prepare students for leadership roles in a complex, interconnected world.
