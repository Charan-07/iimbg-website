Integrated Programme in Management (IPM)

Nurturing young minds into socially conscious and emotionally mature leaders

Program Overview

Launched in 2021, the 5-Year Integrated Programme in Management (IPM) is designed to prepare candidates from a foundation level for taking up leadership positions in corporates and government organizations.

The curriculum offers experiential learning, international immersion, and a holistic course structure. The objective is to provide students with a global perspective towards management practice while instilling ethical judgment and problem-solving skills.

Dual Degree JIPMAT Accepted Exit Option
Dual Degree Award

Upon successful completion of 5 years, students are awarded both BBA and MBA degrees by IIM Bodh Gaya.

Exit Option

Students opting to exit after 3 years are awarded a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) degree.

Social Immersion

A compulsory Rural and Social Immersion internship at the end of the 2nd year to understand ground realities.

Global Perspective

Curriculum designed to foster international understanding and socially conscious leadership.

Course Structure

A rigorous academic journey evolving from foundational concepts to advanced management

First 3 Years (Foundation)

The first three years follow a Semester System. The curriculum focuses on building a strong foundation in:

  • Mathematics & Statistics
  • Economics (Micro & Macro)
  • Psychology & Sociology
  • Literature & Fine Arts
  • Communication Skills
Last 2 Years (Management)

The last two years follow the Trimester System, aligned with the MBA policy. Features include:

  • Core Management Courses
  • Specialization Electives (Sem 6 onwards)
  • Summer Internship (SIP)
  • Master's Thesis / Project
  • International Student Exchange

Admission Process

Admission to the IPM program is primarily through the Joint Integrated Programme in Management Admission Test (JIPMAT) conducted by NTA. The selection process ensures merit-based entry for students who demonstrate aptitude and leadership potential.

Eligibility

10+2 / XII Standard pass with minimum required percentage (usually 60%).

Exam

Valid JIPMAT Score is mandatory for Indian applicants.

Intake

Standard intake involves candidates from diverse backgrounds across India.
