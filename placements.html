Message from the Chairperson

At IIM Bodh Gaya, we have been able to attract talent across the length and breadth of the nation. We ensure that our students are well accustomed to the ever-evolving dynamics of professional corporate culture. Our goal is to use education as a medium to change the world, to have a positive impact on society, and to attain sustainable progress.

We are a young and growing family; within a small span of time, we have shown our commitment to the corporate world and have created an enduring relationship with emerging India. I use this medium to thank our recruitment partners for their valuable support and guidance throughout this journey.

Placement Process

1
Registration

Students register interest (EOI) for job profiles shared by the placement cell.

2
Pre-Placement Talk

Recruiters conduct PPTs to explain organization philosophy and expectations.

3
Shortlisting

Companies review CVs and shortlist suitable candidates for interviews.

4
Interviews

On-campus or off-campus interview rounds are conducted.

5
Final Offer

Successful candidates receive offers. Withdrawals are not permitted post-selection.

  • 1. Registration: Submission of EOI.
  • 2. PPT: Pre-Placement Talks.
  • 3. Shortlisting: CV Review by Recruiters.
  • 4. Interviews: Selection Rounds.
  • 5. Offers: Final selection.
