Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Equipping students for an industry-led career in the global environment

Program Overview

The 2-year Programme in Master of Business Administration (MBA) imparts theoretical and practical erudition of different functional areas of Management.

The programme is designed to enhance our students with a strong awareness and knowledge about the socio-economic environment both at national and global levels. The objective is to bring into being a Global Manager not only with strong analytical and decision-making skills but an overall personality with socially enviable values and ethics.

To achieve this, the curriculum uses a range of audio-visual based teaching methodologies like case studies, real-life problem solving, role-play, group discussion, and computer simulation.

Global Exposure

International Student Exchange Programme with reputed institutes worldwide.

Batch Diversity

Proactive approach to promote equality in gender, ethnicity, and education.

Case Study Method

Pedagogy stresses learning with extensive case study discussions.

Sustainability

Focus on ethical leadership and leaving the planet better for future generations.

Curriculum Structure

Rigorous academic framework designed for holistic development

Structure

The MBA Programme is divided into three segments: Core Courses (First Year), Summer Internship (8 weeks), and Elective Courses (Second Year). An academic year consists of three terms, each spanning 11 to 13 weeks.

Term 1
  • Management Accounting I
  • Organizational Behaviour I
  • Marketing Management I
  • Microeconomics
  • Statistics for Management
  • Information Technology & Systems
  • Business Communication
  • Sustainable Development
Term 2
  • Management Accounting II
  • Financial Management I
  • Organizational Behavior II
  • Mindfulness at Workplace
  • Marketing Management II
  • Macroeconomics
  • Operations Research
  • Design Thinking
Term 3
  • Financial Management II
  • Human Resource Management
  • Marketing Management III
  • Operations Management
  • Business Data Analytics
  • Foreign Language

Scholarships

The following Government of India scholarships are available to eligible MBA students:

  • Central Sector Scholarship Scheme of Top Class Education for Scheduled Castes Students (Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment)
  • Central Sector Scholarship Scheme of Top Class Education for Scheduled Tribes Students (Ministry of Tribal Affairs)
  • Merit-cum means based scholarship scheme for minority communities
  • Scheme of Scholarship for Top Class Education for Students with Disabilities
