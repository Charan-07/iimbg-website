Our Vision, Mission & Values

Vision

To be a globally recognised management school for developing socially responsible and mindful business leaders who create sustainable value.

Mission

To nurture mindful business leaders who combine social responsibility with a global outlook, through strong academic programmes, practical learning and impactful research.

Core Values

  • Mindfulness: Focused awareness and thoughtful action, fostered through reflective practices.
  • Leadership: Authentic, ethical and transparent leadership that builds trust and drives meaningful outcomes.
  • Social Responsibility: Commitment to ethical standards and community engagement for positive societal impact.
  • Sustainability: Working for long-term wellbeing that balances economic viability, social equity and environmental protection.
  • Lifelong Learning: Encouraging curiosity, adaptability and continuous growth across careers and disciplines.

Our Culture

Our culture positions management as a force for good. We aim to balance financial, societal and ecological priorities. Diversity of thought, collaboration, and a spirit of inquiry guide our teaching, research and institutional choices. We work together to innovate and address complex challenges while looking out for each other.

The IIM Bodh Gaya community is committed to creating an inclusive environment where every individual can thrive intellectually, professionally, and personally. Our residential campus fosters deep connections, cross-cultural understanding, and meaningful collaborations that extend far beyond the classroom.

Campus Experience

A vibrant residential community dedicated to holistic development and mindful leadership

Academic Approach

Experiential Learning

We believe in learning by doing. Our curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical application through case studies, simulations, industry projects, and internships.

Research-Driven

Our faculty actively engage in cutting-edge research that contributes to management knowledge while addressing real-world challenges in business and society.

Global Perspective

We foster international partnerships, exchange programs, and diverse cohorts to prepare leaders who can navigate and thrive in a globalized business environment.
